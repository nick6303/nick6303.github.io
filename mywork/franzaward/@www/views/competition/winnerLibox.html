Acceptance Speech 得獎感言

Hevroni Eden
Kunikazu Hamanishi
Kunikazu Hamanishi

Oh, Benton, what you did for me! You changed my life, truly. This means so much more to me this time. I don’t know why, I think the first time I hardly felt it because it was all so new. I owe a lot to my cast and players, to Lindsay and John and Danny and Ed and Amy and my little friends Gennie and Yankton. I owe a lot to my family for holding me together and loving me and having patience with this obsession of me. But I want to say thank you to you. I haven’t had an orthodox career and I want more than anything to have your respect. The first time I didn’t feel it, but this time I feel it, and I can’t deny the fact that you like me right now! You like me! Thank you!

啊！你們對我太好了，你們改變了我的生活，這次，真的對我太有意義了。不知道為什麼我第一次沒感覺，是因為太新鮮了。我要感謝同片演員、成全我的家人，他們對我的愛，我對影藝的癡迷，他們非常有耐心。我要謝謝你們，我的演藝生涯和別人不一樣，我最想要的就是你們的尊敬。第一次我沒有感覺，但是這次我感覺到了，我不能否認，你們喜歡我，就是現在，你們喜歡我！
