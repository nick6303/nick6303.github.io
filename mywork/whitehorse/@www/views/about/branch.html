The Brand
The White Horse logo is symbolic of love; the love between father and son.
“Horse” might be an excellent representation of emerging to prominence

When the co-founder was pondering over what to name the brand, he came across an image of a horse. An idea came to his mind that perhaps “dark horse” might be an excellent representation of emerging to prominence.

Hence, he turned to his son and asked for his opinion. The five-year-old child replied, “I like white horse more than dark one because it is cuter!”. This was it! The brand name, White Horse Ceramic, was decided!

The little story showcases the love of a father for his son, which became our identity in which we aim to help our customers build an ideal dream space for their loved ones.
