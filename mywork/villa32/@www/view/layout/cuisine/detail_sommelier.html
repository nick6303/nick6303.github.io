• Sommelier

    Graduated from the Burgundy Agricultural School (CFPPA) sommelier program in France, in 2009 to obtain the French national sommelier certificate (BREVET PROFESSIONNEL SOMMELIER), for Taiwan's first to obtain the license of the professionals. Is now the chairman of the Taiwan Sommelier Association, but also in the tertiary institutions as a wine lecturer, translated as "my wine life proposal" (three mining culture).
