RELAIS & CHATEAUX
~Since 1954~

Founded in France in 1954, RELAIS & CHATEAUX is a globally recognized top brand; it is designed to promote the unique traditional French lifestyle. So far, the Group has selected 530 top-notch specialty hotels and gourmet restaurants in 64 countries around the world to invite you to enjoy the essence of luxury lifestyle. Its member institutions are independently operated, with both specialty boutique hotels and gourmet restaurants. From the creation of the 8 hotels along the National Road No. 7 from Paris in 1954, it has grown to become an international brand with 530 member institutions in more than 50 countries on five continents. Relais & Châteaux combines traditional essence with modern creativity, natural charm and luxury, and strives to inherit and promote the essence of the true French lifestyle. It is praised as the "Best Hotel Group in the World" by guests.

We believe that the real luxury is to spend enough time to experience the art of natural tranquility and enjoyment. The secret of RELAIS & CHATEAUX is to let you experience a unique experience in a unique location.

RELAIS & CHATEAUX has members all over the world in different styles and colors. There are ancient castles, romantic country manors, charming charms in exotic paradise, mountain huts in the nature reserve, and unique modern art hotels.

The group members have an average of 30 rooms, which guarantees a warm and thoughtful personal service. In order to make your holiday in Relais & Châteaux perfect, all the staff will do their best to meet your every request.

Thanks to its outstanding quality, Travel & Leisure magazine, the Zagat Survey travel guide and other food guides give RELAIS & CHATEAUX and its members a high rating (Group of Foodies) The restaurant has a total of more than 300 Michelin stars.
