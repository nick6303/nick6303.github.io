Only the best, not the second
~Since 2004~

Since its opening in 2004, the San 2 Building, located in Beitou, where the Fengpei Hot Springs are located, does not live in a hotel. Instead, it operates as a “private guest house”. Regardless of whether it is hardware or software, it is based on the principle of "only the best, not the second best", and it is expected to provide customers with the "top class, more honor" enjoyment.

Invisible in the greenery of the mountains, the deep and wide Sanshui pavilion retains nearly two-thirds of the natural landscape of the space, and after the receding of the geothermal valley, the beautiful scenery is natural.

Spring, Wood, Tree, and Stone

Under the plan of the founder Mr. Qiu Minghong, the 32nd and the 2nd halls are composed of four elements: “spring, wood, tree, and stone”, presenting an exquisite design fusion, from design, construction to completion, and insisting on the perfection of creating art products. It took four and a half years to complete. Different from other hotels, the ping effect of the 32nd and 2nd halls is based on “How much pressure can be released from customers per ping”, because we always aim to provide exquisite services and hope to bring unique VIP customer experience.

Most exquisite and honorable service

The entrances of the 32nd and 2nd halls are very low-key and enjoy the natural beauty of spring waterfalls, bird's-swims and lush greenery, which preserves the leisure and tranquility of the city’s secret garden. All the museums have adopted an appointment system. The planning of only 5 rooms is to provide every customer with the most exquisite and honorable service.

Each room is decorated in a unique style. The European style room uses the Bang & Olufsen top-grade audio in Denmark and the world's top class. The maintenance of the brand name Salvatore Ferragamo as a spare table, Puiforcat tableware, Italian designer Boffi's sanitary ware, etc., illustrates the distinguished brand personality of Sanyinhangguan.

Life experience

In 1896, the Japanese native Hiroshi Hirotita set up the first hot spring hotel in Taiwan to open the Taiwanese bathing history; 108 years later, Villa 32 of the 32nd and the second row houses rewritten his life experience.
