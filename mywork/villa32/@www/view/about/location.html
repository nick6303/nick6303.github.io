Location
~Since 2004~

The San 2 Building is located at No. 32 Zhongshan Road, Beitou District. It is recommended that you choose to take the MRT or drive as a means of transportation.

Drive by yourself

Drive straight to Daye Road and go to Zhongshan Road (Dahu House). After you choose the road on the left, you will arrive at the destination about 50 meters.

After the Zhongshan Expressway passes the Taishan toll station, please drive to the direction of Shilin and then transfer to the north and south expressway of the Ring River. Go straight to the Chau Mei Expressway and connect to Daye Road. Daye Road will turn to Zhongshan Road (Dahu House). After the road is selected, the road to the left is about 50 meters to reach the destination.

Take the MRT

You can take the Danshui Line MRT to Beitou Station and take a taxi to the mountain, about 5-8 minutes drive.
